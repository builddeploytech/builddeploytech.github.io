Who We Are

BuildDeploy Tech is a modern digital solutions agency focused on building, deploying, and scaling secure, high-performance digital products for startups, businesses, and growing brands.

The company is founded and led by Sarvesh Singh, a passionate technology professional with hands-on experience across web development, mobile applications, cloud computing, and DevOps automation.

Sarvesh Singh completed his Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) from Roorkee Institute of Technology, Roorkee. During his academic journey, he developed a deep interest in software engineering, cloud platforms, and scalable system design.

After graduation, he professionally trained in Website Development, Mobile App Development, and DevOps & Cloud Engineering. He has worked on real-world production systems involving CI/CD pipelines, cloud infrastructure, automation, monitoring, and performance optimization.

BuildDeploy Tech follows a DevOps-first approach, ensuring every solution is automated, scalable, secure, and future-ready — from clean frontend interfaces to robust backend systems and cloud-native deployments.

Alongside commercial and startup projects, we also handle Government Tenders, JAM/GeM portal projects, and infrastructure deployments, including Active LED and digital display solutions, with full compliance and execution support.

The company is supported by a dedicated IT team comprising experts in web development, mobile app development, DevOps, cloud infrastructure, UI/UX design, and SEO. This collaborative workflow enables faster delivery and reliable outcomes.

Our mission is simple — to deliver practical, scalable, and impact-driven digital solutions that help businesses and organizations grow confidently.
