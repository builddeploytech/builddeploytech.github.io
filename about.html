Who We Are

BuildDeploy Tech is a modern digital solutions agency focused on building, deploying, and scaling secure, high-performance digital products for startups, businesses, and growing brands.

The company is founded and led by Sarvesh Singh, a passionate technology professional with a strong engineering background and hands-on industry experience in web development, mobile applications, cloud computing, and DevOps automation.

Sarvesh Singh completed his Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) from Roorkee Institute of Technology, Roorkee. During his academic journey, he developed a deep interest in software engineering, cloud platforms, and scalable system design.

After graduation, he professionally trained in Website Development, Mobile App Development, and DevOps & Cloud Engineering. He has worked in real-world job roles handling production deployments, CI/CD pipelines, cloud infrastructure, automation, and performance optimization.

BuildDeploy Tech follows a DevOps-first approach, ensuring every solution is automated, scalable, secure, and future-ready. From clean frontend interfaces to robust backend systems and cloud-native deployments, quality remains our top priority.

The company is supported by a dedicated IT team consisting of skilled professionals in web development, mobile app development, DevOps, cloud infrastructure, UI/UX design, and SEO. This collaborative, team-based workflow allows us to deliver faster, smarter, and more reliable solutions.

Whether it’s building a business website, launching a mobile app, deploying cloud infrastructure, or improving search visibility, our mission is simple — to deliver practical, scalable, and impact-driven digital solutions that help businesses grow confidently.
