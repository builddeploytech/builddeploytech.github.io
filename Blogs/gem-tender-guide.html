GeM Portal & Government Tender Guide

Understand GeM registration, bidding and execution of government projects.

Government e-Marketplace (GeM) is an online platform launched by the Government of India to facilitate transparent and efficient procurement of goods and services. This guide explains how businesses can participate in GeM tenders and execute government projects successfully.

What is GeM Portal?

GeM is a unified procurement platform for government departments, PSUs and autonomous bodies to purchase products and services online.

GeM Registration Process

  1. Seller account creation
  2. Business verification & KYC
  3. Product or service listing
  4. Compliance documentation

Bidding & Order Placement

Sellers can participate in bids, BOQs and direct purchases depending on the order value and category.

Key Compliance Requirements

  • GST & PAN compliance
  • Quality certifications
  • Timely delivery & service support

Government IT & Infrastructure Projects

GeM is widely used for IT services, software development, digital infrastructure, Active LED displays and system integration projects.

Best Practices for GeM Success

  • Competitive pricing strategy
  • Accurate product descriptions
  • Timely order fulfillment
  • Professional documentation
